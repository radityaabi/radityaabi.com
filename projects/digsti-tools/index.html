Project Overview

The All-in-One Stimulus Editing Tool is designed to streamline and automate the process of preparing visual materials for surveys. This tool enables users to edit images into proportional shapes automatically, eliminating the need for repetitive manual adjustments.

The primary objective is to reduce time waste caused by manual editing, especially when handling large volumes of images. While traditional manual editing achieves only ~22 images per hour, our tool can process dozens to hundreds of images in just 1–2 minutes. This dramatic efficiency boost ensures faster project delivery, reduced costs, and consistent output quality.

Achievement & Responsibility

  • Efficiency Improvement

    Increased editing speed from 22 images/hour manually to hundreds of images in 1–2 minutes using the automated tool.

  • Automation & Consistency

    Eliminated repetitive manual work by automating proportional shape adjustments, ensuring uniform and high-quality output across all stimuli.

  • Time & Cost Saving

    Reduced project turnaround time significantly, enabling researchers to focus on analysis and insights rather than technical editing tasks.

  • Scalability

    Successfully applied in projects covering 5 countries, proving the tool’s ability to support large-scale, multi-market survey operations.
